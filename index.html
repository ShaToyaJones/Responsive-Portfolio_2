About Me

I was born in Florida; however, I am an "Army Brat" which means that my family moved around a little. I moved back to Florida for college; I've been here since. I am the oldest of three (3). I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. I have a bachelor's degree in Technical Management from DeVry University. I began working on my master's degree in Information Assurance at Strayer University; however, that degree is now on hold because I decided to learn how to code a computer.

I am a member of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Incorporated. I've held leadership positions within this organization such as Phylacter, Secretary, and First Vice President. We do a lot of community service such as hosting community wide baby showers, road clean up, feeding the homeless, etc.
